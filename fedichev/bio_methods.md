 ## Longitudinal analysis of blood markers reveals progressive loss of resilience and predicts human lifespan limit

**Complete Blood Count (CBC):** This is the standard cheap blood test. It measures 
Red blood cells, which carry oxygen
White blood cells, which fight infection
Hemoglobin, the oxygen-carrying protein in red blood cells
Hematocrit, the amount of red blood cells in the blood
Platelets, which help blood to clot

They picked CBC specifically because it's so common that huge databases of it exist (they used the U.S. NHANES dataset and the UK Biobank).
